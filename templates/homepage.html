How does it work...?

According to your need we provide you the best blood bank source around your location. In the case of emergency all you have to do is go to the request section select the emergency form and fill out your details, as per your requirements we find out the nearest blood bank available and provide you with its address. You will also be having alternate areas you can choose any location you like. If you will require some specific blood group frequently then you can find yourself a donor a couple days before you need it. However if you are someone who would like to be a helping hand and donate, you can go to the donate section fill up your details and choose a near by location where you can donate.
